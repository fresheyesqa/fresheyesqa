About Fresh Eyes QA

Fresh Eyes QA was founded in 2017 by Casey Greene. Casey has extensive experience in customer service, support and QA, and brings a very user-focused perspective to quality assurance and engineering.

Fresh Eyes QA was born out of a desire to offer US-based QA to startups, small businesses, independent app developers, and other fine folks on the front lines of development who want to present their best work to the world at affordable prices.

You don't have to do all the work alone, Fresh Eyes QA is here to help.
