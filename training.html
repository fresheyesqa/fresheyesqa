Training

Making the world a better place, one new quality engineer at a time! These trainings are available on an individual basis and for teams.

Training sessions are currently offered as live video sessions or in person at your office, and include screensharing, code samples and Q&A time. Training sessions run about three hours each, with 1-3 hours of outside work required, depending on the course. At the end of the course, you'll also receive a PDF of what was covered, as well as any worksheets or code samples that were covered.

Custom modules can also be developed that are specific to your workflow or current concerns. Trainings may be offered as recorded videos in the future.

Individual trainings are $250, and Team trainings start at $1500 for three people.

So you want to do QA?

Quality Assurance and Quality Engineering are historically a stepping-stone to development, but if you have a curious mind, enjoy leaving things better than you found them, and taking things apart to see how they tick, this is a fantastic field in and of itself. This course will take you through the basics of QA and QE, and show you how to implement these skills in various scenarios.

  • Writing test plans
  • Cross-browser/platform testing
  • Automated tests
  • Exploratory testing
  • Choosing the right test strategy
  • Notes and record-keeping

Adding Quality to your Dev process

QA and Dev don't always get along, but a good relationship between the two teams can result in huge rewards for your company. This training will take you through questions and scenarios that will help you add QA to your existing development process at multiple levels in a cohesive way.

  • Identify current problems
  • Decide on goals and compromises
  • Quality input at all steps in dev process
  • Know your flaws and bramble code
  • Communicating with developers
  • Gatekeepers vs defenders
