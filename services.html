Services

Whether you're just starting out, or have an established company, Fresh Eyes QA has something to offer. Projects start at $500, depending on the complexity of your site or application. Rush options are available at an additional cost.

Project-based QA

Launching something new? Get your new feature reviewed for bugs, user experience and usability before showing it to your customers. Includes a full written report of bugs, issues and recommendations, and adding them directly to your bug tracker if desired.

User Interface Automated Test Suite

Tests are a critical part of development, but they can be a pain to write. Have us build you a set of critical user interface flows that are easy to update, and can be run across browsers using the automated suite of your choice. Tests can be kicked off manually by developers, or added to your CI flow.

QA Sweep

Independent hunt for bugs, accessibility and basic security issues across your site or application. Includes cross-browser and platform checks, as well as UI and usability recommendations. Similar to Project-based QA, but covers your entire site or app, and if done on a recurring basis, can also catch regression issues.

Full Accessibility Audit

Accessibility is becoming more important as the internet becomes more critical in everyone's lives. A / AA / AAA level audit, review of your full website or app using screenreader and other accessibility tools. Includes a full set of recommendations from all A-Levels, as well as other tips for making your sire easier to navigate for all users.
